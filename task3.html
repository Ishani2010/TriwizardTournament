The Third Task

Solve the riddles!

What to do:

Just solve and send the answers in the meeting chat!

Riddle 1: Soft and cuddly, I’ll pull on your heart. But if you only know my last name, I’ll tear you apart. What am I?

Riddle 2: I have two hands but am unable to clap. What am I?

Riddle 3: I have four legs in total, along with a head and a foot. What am I?

Riddle 4: I have no blood pumping through me, but I have four fingers and one thumb. What am I?

Riddle 5: What is as big as an elephant but weighs zero pounds?

Riddle 6: The seven dwarves are named Doc, Grumpy, Happy, Sleepy, Bashful, Sneezy, and Dopey. Name the tallest one in their house.

Riddle 7: There is a place where Friday comes before Thursday. Where is it?

Riddle 8: What’s something that happens twice in a moment, once a minute, but never in one thousand years?
