Overview

Hello my dear student! This is the Triwizard Tournament page. Here you will be able to access all the tasks you need to complete in order to win the Triwizard Cup. In this Tournament, a champion will be chosen from different Magical Institutes. The Goblet of Fire will choose the champions. The champion will have to go through a series of tasks which will be challenging. So if you want to add your name to the Goblet of Fire, then don't waste any time!
